Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about LÎLO Club

How many experiences can I book per month?

It depends on your membership tier: Explorer (2/month), Insider (5/month), VIP (unlimited). Some premium experiences may count as 2 slots.

Can I bring guests who aren't members?

Yes! Insider members can bring 1 guest per month, VIP members can bring 2 guests per month. Additional guests can be added for a small fee.

What happens if I need to cancel?

You can cancel your membership anytime with no penalty. For individual experiences, cancellation 48+ hours before gets you a credit. Less than 48 hours is non-refundable.

Are experiences only on weekends?

We offer experiences 7 days a week! Weekday options are often less crowded. Use our calendar filter to find options that match your schedule.

How do you select experience hosts?

We have a rigorous vetting process. Hosts must be passionate locals with authentic knowledge. Many are grandmothers, fishermen, artists, and historians who don't normally offer commercial tours.
